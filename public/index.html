About-us

We provide great services and ideass

Shambhu Dayal Sr. Sec. School is one of the oldest, co-educational schools in the district Sonipat. It offers its students and staff a pristine environment for learning and developing in all spheres of life.

At Sambhu Dyal School we believe education should empower children to live a balanced happy life. We endeavor to build strong character that helps students understand and appreciate the right values.

The school endeavors to keep abreast with the latest technologies in education. Various cultural activities are organized to establish a meaningful connect with the vast and glorious heritage of our country. The school stands out for not only giving wings, but also keeping the children grounded by inculcating morals, ethics, values and respect for our rich culture.
